Welcome to the Prison Game Mode!

In this game mode, players will experience the thrill of prison life. You can interact with other players, trade items, and complete missions to earn your freedom.

Game Features

  • Realistic prison environment
  • In-depth interaction with NPCs
  • Various missions to complete
  • Trading system among players

Getting Started

To get started, follow these steps:

  1. Join a server supporting the Prison game mode.
  2. Create your character and customize your look.
  3. Start your prison life by completing missions or interacting with other players!
