Lifesteal Gamemode

Welcome to the Lifesteal gamemode! In this game, players can steal health from others while combating in the arena.

Rules:

  • When you defeat an opponent, you gain a portion of their health.
  • If you die, you will respawn with reduced health.
  • Be strategic and choose your battles wisely!

How to Play:

  1. Join the game lobby.
  2. Select your character and prepare for battle.
  3. Engage with other players and use your skills to survive!
