Survival Gamemode

Welcome to Survival Mode

In this mode, players must gather resources, craft items, and survive against various challenges.

Objectives

  • Gather resources from the environment
  • Craft tools and shelters
  • Survive against enemies and natural hazards

Tips for Survival

  1. Always be aware of your surroundings.
  2. Prioritize gathering food and shelter.
  3. Explore to find valuable resources.
